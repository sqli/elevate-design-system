## 1.2 Logotype

The *s* is warm, soft, elegant and humanistic. The simple verticality of *l* and *i* incorporates pure elevation. The *q* combines both: beautiful curves and straight geometry.

**When we refer to the company, SQLI is always written in upper case.**

## 1.4 Typeface

Our brand typeface is **TWK Everett**.

Everett is simple, clean, and calm -- rooted in timeless modernist typography. It carries a refined elegance with subtle, distinctive touches, like the clipped tops of its diagonals.

We use 3 different weights -- **Light**, **Regular** and **Medium**.

We generally suggest using **TWK Everett Light for body text**.

## 1.5 Brand Colours

If we elevate, we might reach the sky.

We use this metaphor to define our new color palette where three shades of blue play the main role.

The primary colour scheme includes a background in **Cream** with type and graphical elements set in **Midnight Blue**. Highlights can be emphasised with **Sky Blue** or **Cobalt Blue**.

**Cobalt Blue** is our primary interaction colour -- use it for links, buttons, controls and other clickable elements. Cobalt and Sky Blue should be treated as a complementary colour in our brand.

## 1.6 Promise

Every brand's claim is a neat, simple summary of what the brand does for its clients and its people.

### Rules of use

- In our promise, there's always **a full stop after "Elevate."** -- never anywhere else.
- The promise should always **complement the logo, never overshadowing it**.
- The brand promise can be presented in **two lines** when it suits the format and available space.
- We write **Elevate. Digitally** in TWK Everett Regular.

### Presentation formats

**Inline -- text only**

> Elevate. Digitally

**Inline -- with Ascenders**

> [Ascenders] Elevate. Digitally

**Two-line -- text only**

> Elevate.
> Digitally

**Two-line -- with Ascenders**

> [Ascenders] Elevate.
>             Digitally
