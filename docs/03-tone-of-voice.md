# 3. Tone of Voice

> Retour au [sommaire](./README.md) | Precedent : [Usage Guide](./02-usage-guide.md)

Our tone of voice is **Professionally Human**.

## 3.1 Talking. Generally.

We're a group of selected, highly-trained, intelligent experts dedicated to finding digital solutions that elevate business sectors, anywhere on Earth.

In short, we're professional.

Yet unlike many digital businesses, we don't work in silos, or in fixed processes, or in a set hierarchy. We're not a digital machine. We're a team of people.

In other words, we're human.

So that in mind, the way we talk, both to each other and the whole world is:

**Professionally Human.**

At SQLI we're all professionally human, so talking this way is natural. But because we work in different sectors and places, here's how we can all talk as one.

## 3.2 Talking. Emotionally.

A few words about the way we talk, rather than how.

### Elevate. Personally.

At SQLI, elevating is a culture rather than a product. Every day we elevate each other, our relationships, each other's sense of worth, even our hopes.

So every time you write, make your reader feel more uplifted, more optimistic even prouder, than when you started. Speak up to them, as if they always know more than you do. And leave them on top of the world.

### Elevate. Humanly.

Many businesses in our sector write like their technology. It's technical and businesslike, yet impersonal. And SQLI is an interconnected team of people.

So write more like you'd talk. Imagine a conversation with a colleague or a close friend. Someone on your level.

That in mind, be simple. Avoid using business cliches like "solution-oriented workflows" and "agile corporate adaptability". Instead, prove you're part of a team of people rather than a machine. It's what makes SQLI different. And you.

### Elevate. Simply.

Sometimes, the way a digital solution works is beyond even the people who want to use one. Try to explain even complex issues in a way anyone could understand.

### Elevate. Elegantly.

SQLI takes care. Everything we do is considered. Carefully executed, planned in advance. The design guide's a good example. It's like comparing some handmade furniture to stuff you'd buy in a discount store. It says SQLI considers, and takes more care.

Do the same when you write. When you're done, craft again. Adjust. Refine.

### Elevate. Interestingly.

Don't forget to add a little bit of yourself to everything you write. A little lateral thought. A point of view. You're part of the team, too.

## 3.3 Word Choices

Don't just force them in because you feel you have to. But when you see an opportunity to use one, build it into your line or phrase. You'll be contributing to a culture every time.

You'll see that corporate cliches like "raise the bar", "dial up" and "head for new heights" aren't on the list. It's because they're used so often, they don't mean much now. Leave them out.

Instead, if you can think of some new ones, feel free. Remember we're a company of thinkers.

### Preferred vocabulary

- **Everyday**
- Up. Higher.
- Lift. Pick up.
- Raise. Raising. Uplift.
- Elevate. Elevating.
- Inspire. Inspiring.
- Augment. Amplify.
- Bolster. Increase. Increasing.
- Look up. Transform.
- Go skywards. Never look back.
- Reach for the moon.
- **Now and again.**

Some professionally human alternatives:

| ~~Cliche~~ | Preferred alternative |
|------------|----------------------|
| ~~Actionable~~ | **Doable, feasible, possible** |
| ~~Backburner~~ | **Keep in mind** |
| ~~Blue Sky Thinking~~ | **New ideas** |
| ~~Communicate~~ | **Say, talk, discuss** |
| ~~Circle Back~~ | **Let you all know** |

| ~~Get on board~~ | **Join** |
| ~~Gain Traction~~ | **Attract interest** |
| ~~Ideate~~ | **Think** |
| ~~Leverage~~ | **Make use of** |
| ~~Paradigm shift~~ | **Fundamental change** |
| ~~Journey~~ | **Way to reach it** |
| ~~Worldwide~~ | **Across the world** |
| ~~Reach out~~ | **Get in touch** |
