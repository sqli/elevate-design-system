## 4.1 WCAG 2.1 Compliance

Our SQLI colour palette is fully **AAA WCAG 2.1 compliant**, reflecting our commitment to accessibility and inclusivity. It's a detail that underscores our dedication to creating digital experiences that are not only beautiful but also universally accessible -- because elevating digitally means elevating for everyone.

| Combination | Contrast Ratio | Normal Text | Large Text | Graphical Objects & UI Components |
|-------------|---------------|-------------|------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Midnight Blue and Cream** | 18.05:1 | AAA | AAA | AA |
| **Black and White** | 18.7:1 | AAA | AAA | AA |
| **Cobalt Blue and Cream** | 8.11:1 | AAA | AAA | AA |
| **Midnight Blue and Sky Blue** | 7.58:1 | AAA | AAA | AA |

## 4.2 Logo Colour Guide

We use accessible colour contrast principles to determine what colour combinations are permitted for logo application. These apply when the company name is not written alongside the logo.

Examples marked "Approved" are all AAA accessible and approved for use.
